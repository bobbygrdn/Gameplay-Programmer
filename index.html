About Me

I have been a gamer my entire life. I started playing video games on the old Atari 2600 playing my first game... Pitfall. Ever since then I have played every console I could get my hands on and found one of my true passions through the storytelling of the gaming world. My all time favorite games are Action RPGs. Some of my favorite titles are the Mass Effect Series, the Assassin's Creed series, and the Deus Ex series.

I started my programming journey as a Front-End Developer which turned into me becoming a Full-Stack Web Developer. Unable to find my true home, I turned to the gaming industry and have trained relentlessly to become a Gameplay Engineer. My jouney has taken me through several programming languages, several game engines and a few Game Jams.

My goal is to be a valuable asset to a game studio who wants to bring the best form of storytelling to life and create a product that gamers everywhere will love for years to come.
