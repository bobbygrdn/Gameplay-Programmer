Unity Version: 2021.3.28f1

Game Type: Platformer

Type of Project: Game Jam

Live Game Link

  • Helped design and implement the player controls for flying the sleigh and dropping presents. Our goal was to create intuitive and responsive controls that provided a smooth gameplay experience.
  • Developed various gameplay elements such as obstacles, power-ups, and scoring systems. This involved designing different types of chimneys or houses, varying weather conditions, or special presents that give bonus points.
  • Played a crucial role in testing and balancing the game. This involved adjusting the difficulty level, tuning the physics, or modifying the game mechanics based on player feedback to ensure the game was fun and challenging.
  • Worked closely with the design and art teams to ensure the game mechanics aligned with the visual and narrative aspects of the game. This collaboration was essential to create a cohesive and immersive game experience.

About Me

I have been a gamer my entire life. I started playing video games on the old Atari 2600 playing my first game... Pitfall. Ever since then I have played every console I could get my hands on and found one of my true passions through the storytelling of the gaming world. My all time favorite games are Action RPGs. Some of my favorite titles are the Mass Effect Series, the Assassin's Creed series, and the Deus Ex series.

I started my programming journey as a Front-End Developer which turned into me becoming a Full-Stack Web Developer. Unable to find my true home, I turned to the gaming industry and have trained relentlessly to become a Gameplay Engineer. My jouney has taken me through several programming languages, several game engines and a few Game Jams.

My goal is to be a valuable asset to a game studio who wants to bring the best form of storytelling to life and create a product that gamers everywhere will love for years to come.
